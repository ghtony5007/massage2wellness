Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our booking system, call us at (555) 123-4567, or visit us in person. Online booking is available 24/7 for your convenience.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete intake forms. We'll discuss your needs and preferences, then guide you to a private room for your massage.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are perfect for birthdays, holidays, or any special occasion. They can be purchased online or in person.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and digital payments. Payment is due at the time of service.

Is gratuity expected?

Gratuity is appreciated but not required. The standard range is 15-20% of the service cost, but any amount is welcome.
